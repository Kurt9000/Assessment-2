About Wuthering Waves

Wuthering Waves is a visually stunning, narrative-driven action RPG set in a post-apocalyptic world where civilization is rebuilding after a mysterious disaster known as the “Lament.” Players take on the role of Rovers, explorers with unique powers who awaken with no memories, and must uncover the secrets of the world and their own identities.

The game features fast-paced combat with fluid combo mechanics, character-switching, and special resonance skills. Players can collect and build a team of distinct characters, each with their own backstories, elemental affinities, and weapons. The open world offers vertical exploration, dynamic environments, and a rich lore woven into every region and quest.

Developed by Kuro Game, known for the acclaimed title Punishing: Gray Raven, Wuthering Waves blends gorgeous anime-style visuals with a deep and immersive gameplay experience that rewards both exploration and mastery of combat mechanics.
